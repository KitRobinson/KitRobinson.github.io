Welcome the the Chronicle of Palomar!

What is the Chronicle of Palomar?

The Chronicle of Palomar is an forum based game, where players take on the role of a deity in the world of Palomar. As players you will guide your worshippers, shape the world, and engage in heavenly diplomacy - but choose your allies carefully!

How do I create a character?

Character creation occurs in three stages, but before we begin, you should have an outline for your deity - this will help you have an overall concept, help me to ajudicate any special rules needed, and also will allow me to follow through on the basic plan, should you be unable to participate in one of these steps.

Deities have three basic things which define them:

  1. Portfolios
  2. Deific Powers
  3. Deific Stats

Portfolios

A portfolio is an area of responsibility for your deity - your portfolios define what you are the god of. In greek mythology for example, Poseidon was the god of the sea, and of horses - so Poseidon would have the Sea and Horses portfolios. Each portfolio has a minimum point cost, but you can pay more if you like - the more points invested in a portfolio, the more effect it will have in the world of Palomar. Here are some examples:
